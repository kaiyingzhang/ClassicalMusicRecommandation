Compare this approach to say, Chopin and you will see the connection. The fugues on the other hand would be played more straight due to their musical  structure so that the contrapuntal interweaving of the voices could be clearly heard. Of course there are various interpretive choices ( I as an organist myself play this work with more freedom than i used to) but the above is
is a pretty much respected norm.
